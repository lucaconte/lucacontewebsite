Luca Conte (a.k.a. “Mario incandenza”, “incandenza”, “3ll3c1”) is a nerd by deep and intimate vocation. He forges fluid ecologies with machines, coaxing circuits and code into sensorial mirages. Modular synthesis, creative coding, audiovisual installations, and latent diffusion — his tools are not instruments, but partners in error and emergence.
